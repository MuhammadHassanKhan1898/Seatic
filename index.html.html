INTRODUCTION

SEATIC TMK was created in 2018 with the intention to sparkle the streak of motivation in the students of TMK. Its only objective is to uplift the aspirations in the students of TMK in their academic career. It patternizes the tests of different institutes of Pakistan. It is the only non-profit organisation in TMK which prepares quick sample tests of the business, engineering, medical, and Law institutions of Pakistan. And those institutions are IBA Karachi ( business school), National engineering University (NED), Jinnah Medical college and etcetera. Apart from conducting aptitude tests, SEATIC has arranged other projects like career counseling sessions, motivational lectures for the students of TMK and it has accomplished them successfully.

This organisation is merely designed to inspire the scholars and to assist them achieve their desired goals. That's why this organisation has the people who themselves are the students of some well known universities of Pakistan which also makes this organisation unique and special from other organizations of TMK. The journey of from being known to no one to known to many, is itself very motivating and encouraging. SEATIC promises for the betterment of its projects that it can bring for you the peeps of TMK.
